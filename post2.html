Beauty of Flowers

Lotus

The lotus flower is a symbol of purity and rebirth. It grows in muddy water but rises above the surface to bloom with remarkable beauty. Lotus flowers come in various colors, including white, pink, and yellow.

Jasmine

Jasmine flowers are known for their sweet fragrance and delicate white petals. They are often used in perfumes and teas, and in some cultures, jasmine represents love and beauty.

The Vibrance of Green Flowers

Green flowers, like the hydrangea, bring a unique and refreshing vibrance to gardens and floral arrangements. Their color represents harmony, balance, and a connection with nature. Green flowers are a symbol of health, resilience, and the beauty of new growth.
