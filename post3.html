Creeper Flowers

Bougainvillea

Bougainvillea is a vibrant creeper plant known for its bright, papery bracts that come in shades of pink, purple, red, and orange. It thrives in warm climates and is often used to cover fences, walls, and trellises.

Morning Glory

Morning Glory is a fast-growing creeper with trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the morning and close by afternoon. The flowers are typically blue, pink, or purple, and they are known for their ability to quickly cover surfaces.

Passionflower

Passionflower is a unique and intricate creeper flower, often used for its medicinal properties. The flowers are striking, with a complex structure and a range of colors including purple, blue, and white. It is also the source of the passionfruit.
