Tulip

Tulips are spring-blooming perennials that grow from bulbs. They are known for their striking and colorful blooms, which come in almost every color of the rainbow. Tulips are a favorite in gardens and floral arrangements.

They require well-drained soil and full sunlight to thrive. Tulips can often be seen blooming in early spring and are a popular choice for adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
